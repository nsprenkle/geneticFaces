Face Drawing - Genetic Algorithm Test

Welcome! This is the page for my project on face generation and genetic algorithms.

This program draws a series of faces. Rate all the faces on their attractiveness, then click "Next Generation" at the bottom of the page.

Using a variant of the Simple Genetic Algorithm (SGA), this program will create new generations of faces passing along favorable elements.
